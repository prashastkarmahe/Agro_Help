Drone-Farming

Drones can help farmers to optimize the use of inputs (seed, fertilizers, water), to react more quickly to threats (weeds, pests, fungi), to save time crop scouting, to improve variable-rate prescriptions in real time and estimate yield from a field.

Precision Agriculture

Precision agriculture is a management strategy that gathers, processes and analyzes data and combines it with other information to support management decisions according to estimated variability for improved resource use and agricultural production.

Aeroponics

Aeroponics is the process of growing plants in an air or mist environment without the use of soil or an aggregate medium.
